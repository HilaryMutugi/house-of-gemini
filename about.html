Our story

Designed for performance. Tailored for distinction.

House of Gemini is a founder-led uniform design and manufacturing enterprise based at Pearl Crescent Drive, Kamulu, Nairobi, with Mariakani as its gateway to Kenya’s coastal region.

Where it began

Raising the standard of locally made uniforms.

House of Gemini was created to close a clear gap: institutions need a local partner who can combine dependable production with premium finishing, consistent sizing and integrated branding.

From its Nairobi base, the brand serves clients across Kenya. Through Mariakani, it remains closely connected to schools, industries and organizations throughout Mombasa, Kilifi and the wider coastal corridor.

From the founder

“House of Gemini is built to make uniforms that perform well, Ankara pieces that celebrate who we are, and lasting opportunities through local manufacturing.”

— Anita Thuo, Founder
