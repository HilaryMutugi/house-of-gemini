How it works

A clear journey from your idea to delivery.

Custom clothing should feel exciting, not confusing. We guide each project through a simple, transparent process.

01

Inquiry

Tell us what you need, who it is for, your estimated quantity, preferred date and any early ideas.

02

Consultation & quote

We discuss your goals, practical needs, budget and timeline, then prepare the appropriate order scope and quotation.

03

Design & sample approval

Together we confirm style, colours, fabric, branding and finishing. A sample may be developed for approval before bulk production.

04

Measurements & sizing

We confirm individual measurements or agree on a size range and collection method for group orders.

05

Deposit & production

Once the approved details and deposit are received, materials are secured and your garments move into structured production.

06

Fitting & quality check

Where needed, we arrange a fitting. Every finished piece is checked for fit, finish and order accuracy.

07

Delivery

Your completed order is prepared for collection or agreed delivery, ready to be worn with pride.

Timing

Good work needs a well-planned timeline.

Lead times vary by garment complexity, fabric availability and order size. Early inquiries give us more room to source well, test details and deliver calmly.
